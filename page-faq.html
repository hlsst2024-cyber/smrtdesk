Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SmrtDesk, our review process, and how we help you find the best products for your home and office.

1 About SmrtDesk

What is SmrtDesk

SmrtDesk is an independent product review and recommendation platform. We research, test, and compare home and office products to help you make informed buying decisions. Our team of expert testers spends hours evaluating each product before it earns our recommendation. Unlike other review sites, we never accept payment for positive reviews — every pick is earned through hands-on testing.

How does SmrtDesk make money

SmrtDesk earns a small commission when you purchase a product through our affiliate links. This comes at no extra cost — you pay the same price whether you use our link or go directly. Our editorial independence is absolute: we never accept paid placements, sponsored reviews, or free products in exchange for positive coverage. Our recommendations are based solely on expert testing and research.

Who is behind SmrtDesk

SmrtDesk is built by a team of product enthusiasts, researchers, and industry experts passionate about helping consumers find the best value. Our team includes former furniture designers, consumer electronics reviewers, home improvement specialists, and digital publishing veterans. We bring decades of combined product testing experience to every review.

2 Our Review Process

How do you test products

Our testing process is rigorous and varies by product category, but always includes extensive real-world use. For standing desks, we assemble each unit, use it as our primary desk for a minimum of 20 hours, and evaluate build quality, ease of assembly, performance, stability at multiple heights, motor noise, and overall value. Each product is scored across 8?C12 criteria before receiving a final rating.

How many products have you tested

We have tested over 50 products across furniture, storage, electronics, and home categories. Our testing library grows every month as we acquire and evaluate new products. We prioritize the most popular, best-selling, and highly-rated products on the market to ensure our coverage is relevant to what real shoppers are considering.

How often do you update reviews

We review our product recommendations quarterly and update them whenever new models are released, prices change significantly, or we discover new information that affects a product's rating. Each review page displays an "Updated" badge with the latest review date so you always know the information is current.

Can I trust your ratings

Absolutely. Our ratings are based on a consistent scoring framework applied across all products. We do not accept free products, sponsored placements, or payment for positive reviews. Every product we recommend has been purchased, assembled, tested, and evaluated by our team. If a product does not meet our standards, it does not receive a recommendation — regardless of any relationship with the manufacturer.

3 Shopping on SmrtDesk

Can I buy products directly on SmrtDesk

No, SmrtDesk does not sell products directly. We are a review and recommendation platform, not a retailer. When you find a product you like, click the "Check Price on Amazon" button or any of our affiliate links to be redirected to the retailer's site where you can complete your purchase.

How do I find the best price

Each product page shows the current price range from major retailers. We highlight deals, discounts, and promotions in our Best Deals section. Prices can fluctuate, so clicking through to the retailer gives you the most up-to-date pricing and any active promotions.

Do you offer price matching

No, we do not offer price matching. We display prices available at the time of publishing. Since prices can change daily, we always recommend clicking through to verify the current price at the retailer before making your purchase.

Is my personal information safe when I click your links

Yes. When you click an affiliate link on SmrtDesk, you are redirected directly to the retailer's website. We do not collect or store any personal information from these clicks. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more details on how we handle data.
