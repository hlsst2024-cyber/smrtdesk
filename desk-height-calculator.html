Frequently Asked Questions

What is the standard standing desk height?

There is no single "standard" — standing desk height depends on your height. For someone 170 cm (5'7"), the ideal standing height is about 107 cm. Use the calculator above for your exact measurement.

How do I know if my desk is at the right height?

When seated, your forearms should be parallel to the floor with elbows at 90° while typing. When standing, the same rule applies — your wrists should be straight, not bent up or down.

Can I use this calculator for an L-shaped standing desk?

Yes! The height recommendations apply regardless of desk shape. If you use a keyboard tray, subtract the tray height from the desk height recommendation.

What about monitor height?

The top of your monitor should be at or slightly below eye level. This calculator gives you the height from the floor to the top edge of your screen when seated.
