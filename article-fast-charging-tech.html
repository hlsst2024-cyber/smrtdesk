What is Fast Charging

Fast charging refers to technologies that deliver higher wattage to your devices, significantly reducing charge times. Modern standards include USB-C Power Delivery (PD), Qualcomm Quick Charge, and GaN (Gallium Nitride) technology that allows smaller, cooler chargers at higher power outputs.

GaN Chargers vs Traditional

GaN chargers are the biggest breakthrough in charging tech. They run cooler, are significantly smaller, and can deliver higher wattage than traditional silicon-based chargers. Our top pick is the AUKEY 108W USB-C Charger Hub, which can charge a laptop, tablet, and phone simultaneously at full speed.

Wireless Charging Pads

Modern wireless charging has improved dramatically. Look for pads that support at least 15W fast charging for compatible phones. Key features: coil alignment, cooling fans, multi-device charging, and stand vs pad form factor.

Power Banks for Travel

For portable charging, capacity and output matter most. A 20,000mAh power bank with USB-C PD 45W output can charge a laptop to 50% in under an hour. Look for units with pass-through charging and airplane-safe certifications.
